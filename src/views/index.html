Shopping Cart App

This is a personal project made by Jose Abraham Castillo whose goal was to learn some modern topics of PHP like: MVC (Model-View-Controller) pattern, routing, working with sessions, namespaces, composer, a template engine called Twig and tie it all together with a little of modern JavaScript.

It is a basic app where you can login/register, select from a list of products and then perform a "payment" process, you can also rate items from 1 to 5 stars.

I did this long time ago as a challenge for a job interview and now I made a complete refactor to teach myself some modern concepts, the app still has some of the rules it had for the interview:
1. You have a $100 budget every session. And 2. You can rate a product only once per session.

Please feel free to see, use, fork, critique, suggest or help in any way.
